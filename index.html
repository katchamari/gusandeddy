WHAT IS GUS & EDDY?

Gus & Eddy is a weekly show starring best friends Gus Johnson and Eddy Burback, where they goof back and forth with witty commentary. The podcast occasionally features many other YouTubers, actors, and musicians such as Drew Gooden, Michael Reeves, Jacksfilms, NakeyJakey, and more! Tune in for segments such as opening mail, answering questions and resolving disagreements from fans, and Gus & Eddy related business updates!

MEET THE BOYS

GUS JOHNSON is a YouTube content creator from a small town in Wisconsin. He moved to L.A. with his best friend Eddy Burback and is most known for his short-form sketch comedy with eccentric characters, his longer commentary videos, and his music.

WHAT IS A BOY?

Gus & Eddy have a universally loved slogan: Boys Support Boys. But, what is a boy? Boys are what Gus & Eddy call their viewers-- anyone can be a boy! The title 'boy' exists regardless of gender and is imbued into every Gus & Eddy viewer at birth. Every boy has a duty to support and love their boys.
